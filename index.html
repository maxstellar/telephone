HOW IT WORKS

01🤫

Get secretly assigned a team (or up to 3 teams)

02🧩

On your turn, work on your game over the course of 4 days!

03🛫

Ship your contributions + receive small prizes!

04🤝

We'll pass your game to the next person

05🎉

After your team's game is done, finally meet your team + discuss to receive a final prize!
